Stephen McDonald
----------------

Stephen McDonald is a software engineer who has worked in the web development
space since the late 90s. He currently works for Google, and is the creator of
Mezzanine, one of the most popular CMS projects for the Django framework.

Loïc Bistuer
------------

Loïc studied telecommunications engineering and works as an independent software
developer and consultant.  He discovered Django in 2008 shortly before the 1.0
release and has been hooked ever since. He contributes mostly to Django’s ORM
and Form components.
